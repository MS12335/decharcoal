Cube Briquettes

Charcoal briquettes are cube-shaped fuel sources made from compressed charcoal dust, sawdust, or other wood materials. They are commonly used for grilling and cooking as a convenient and consistent heat source. Charcoal briquettes are known for their long-lasting burn and steady heat, making them a popular choice for barbecues, outdoor cooking, and even some indoor cooking applications. They are a favorite among grill enthusiasts for their ease of use and ability to maintain a stable cooking temperature.
